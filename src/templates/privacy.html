Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This privacy policy describes how AI Chatbot ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service and its Google Calendar integration.

Data We Collect

When you connect your Google account, the App accesses:

  • Google Calendar events - event titles, times, descriptions, attendees, and calendar metadata
  • Email address - your Google account email for identification
  • User profile - basic profile information associated with your Google account

How We Use Your Data

  • Calendar events are displayed in the App's planner dashboard and used by the AI assistant to help manage your schedule
  • Your email address is used to identify your connected Google account within the App
  • Data is processed locally to provide personalized scheduling assistance

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the server running the App
  • OAuth tokens (access and refresh tokens) are stored in the local database to maintain your Google Calendar connection
  • Calendar data is cached locally for performance (1-hour TTL)
  • Your data is not shared with any third parties
  • No data is sold, rented, or otherwise distributed to external services

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account exists and your Google Calendar is connected
  • When you disconnect Google Calendar, all stored tokens and cached calendar data are immediately deleted

Revoking Access

You can revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time:

  • Within the App: Go to Settings and click "Disconnect" next to Google Calendar
  • Via Google: Visit Google Account Permissions and remove access for this app

Security

  • All communication with Google APIs uses HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0 is used for authentication (the App never sees your Google password)
  • Access tokens are short-lived and automatically refreshed

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact the App administrator.
